DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NexGenSpec is the professional home inspection app built for modern inspectors. Conduct thorough property inspections, document defects with annotated photos, and deliver polished PDF reports to your clients — all from a single app.

INSPECT WITH CONFIDENCE
Create structured inspections with customizable section templates (Roof, Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC, and more). Each item tracks severity levels (Safety, Major, Marginal, Minor), observations, implications, and recommendations.

CAPTURE & ANNOTATE
Take photos directly in the app and mark them up with arrows, circles, and PencilKit drawings. Annotations are baked into report images while originals are preserved as untouched evidence.

PROFESSIONAL PDF REPORTS
Generate professional inspection reports with your company logo, name, and license number. Reports include a defect summary with Safety and Major findings highlighted, photo evidence laid out side-by-side, signatures, and a unique Report ID for cross-referencing.

LIDAR ROOM SCANNING
Capture 3D room scans using your device's LiDAR sensor. USDZ models are saved alongside the inspection for dimensional reference.

INVOICE & SEND
Fill out an invoice, attach the PDF report, and email everything to your client directly from the app. Professional HTML email templates make every communication look polished.

BUILT FOR SECURITY
- All data stored on-device with iOS Data Protection
- Encrypted backup and restore
- Sign in with Apple or email/password via Firebase Auth
- Finalized reports are locked and hash-verified
- Delete account permanently removes all data

APP REVIEW NOTES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Demo Account:
  Email: appreview@nexgenspec.com
  Password: NexGen-Review-2026!

  To test the full app flow:
  1. Log in with the demo account above (or create a new account — both work).
  2. Accept the Privacy Policy + Terms in the onboarding flow.
  3. On the Dashboard, tap "+ New Inspection" and fill in a client name + property address.
  4. In the inspection's left sidebar, tap any section (Roof Structure, Plumbing, etc.) to see the built-in checklist of items.
  5. Tap an item → mark Status, set Defect Severity, fill in Observed/Implication/Recommendation, add a photo, optionally annotate with the pencil tools.
  6. Optional: tap "Add Custom Item" at the bottom of any section list to add a bespoke check.
  7. Tap "Preview" in the top-right toolbar to see the PDF preview anytime.
  8. To finalize: sidebar → Finalize → Collect Signatures (both inspector and client) → Finalize & Lock. Once locked, defects/photos/signatures cannot be edited.
  9. After finalizing, the "Invoice & Send" pane appears in the sidebar — fill the invoice and send via the system Mail composer.

Notes for Reviewer:
- This is a professional home inspection app for licensed property inspectors.
- The app requires login to protect inspection data (client PII, property photos).
- Free users get 3 complete inspections with full PDF export. After 3 inspections, a Pro subscription ($49/mo or $449/yr) is required to continue creating new inspections.
- LiDAR room scanning requires a device with a LiDAR sensor (iPad Pro, iPhone Pro). If the device has no LiDAR sensor the feature is hidden — this is expected.
- Company logo upload (Settings → Inspector Profile) brands every PDF report. If no logo is uploaded, the NexGenSpec app icon is used as a default.
- Calendar integration is optional. The first time the inspector taps "Add to Calendar" on an inspection, iOS prompts for Calendar access. Granting the permission lets the app create / update / remove EKEvents linked to that inspection. Declining the permission disables only this feature; the rest of the app works unchanged.
- The "Mark Invoice as Paid" button on the Invoice & Send screen is purely a record-keeping toggle. NexGenSpec does NOT process payments. The Invoice & Send screen carries an explicit disclaimer: "NexGenSpec does not process payments. Payment is collected directly by the inspector outside the app."
- The "Delete Account" option is in Settings → Delete Account, with confirmation and re-authentication. It permanently removes all user data from Firebase Auth and the device.
- Subscriptions are auto-renewable and managed through the App Store. Users can cancel anytime in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
- The app stores all inspection data on-device using iOS Data Protection. The only data sent off-device is: (a) Firebase Auth tokens for sign-in, (b) anonymized crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics. Inspection content (photos, defects, client info) never leaves the device unless the inspector chooses to email a PDF report.
