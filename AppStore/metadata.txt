DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NexGenSpec is the professional home inspection app built for modern inspectors. Conduct thorough property inspections, document defects with annotated photos, and deliver polished PDF reports to your clients — all from a single app.

INSPECT WITH CONFIDENCE
Create structured inspections with customizable section templates (Roof, Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC, and more). Each item tracks severity levels (Safety, Major, Marginal, Minor), observations, implications, and recommendations.

CAPTURE & ANNOTATE
Take photos directly in the app and mark them up with arrows, circles, and PencilKit drawings. Annotations are baked into report images while originals are preserved as untouched evidence.

PROFESSIONAL PDF REPORTS
Generate professional inspection reports with your company logo, name, and license number. Reports include severity badges, photo evidence, signatures, and a SHA-256 verification hash for tamper-proof delivery.

HANDS-FREE VOICE COMMANDS (PRO)
Use voice commands during inspections: "Next room", "Add note cracked foundation", "Defect: broken window". Stay focused on the inspection while NexGenSpec handles the documentation.

LIDAR ROOM SCANNING
Capture 3D room scans using your device's LiDAR sensor. USDZ models are saved alongside the inspection for dimensional reference.

INVOICE & SEND
Fill out an invoice, attach the PDF report, and email everything to your client directly from the app. Professional HTML email templates make every communication look polished.

BUILT FOR SECURITY
- All data stored on-device with iOS Data Protection
- Encrypted backup and restore
- Sign in with Apple or email/password via Firebase Auth
- Finalized reports are locked and hash-verified
- Delete account permanently removes all data

SUBSCRIPTION
NexGenSpec is free to download with 3 full-featured inspections. Upgrade to Pro for:
- Unlimited inspections
- PDF reports with your company logo
- Voice commands
- LiDAR room scanning
- Annotation pack
- Invoicing & client delivery

Pro: $28.99/month or $289.99/year

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Notes for Reviewer:
- This is a professional home inspection app for licensed property inspectors.
- The app requires login to protect inspection data (client PII, property photos).
- Free users get 3 complete inspections with full PDF export. After 3 inspections, a Pro subscription ($28.99/mo or $289.99/yr) is required to continue creating new inspections.
- Voice commands require microphone and speech recognition permissions, requested at the time of use (not on launch).
- LiDAR scanning requires a device with a LiDAR sensor (iPad Pro, iPhone Pro). If no LiDAR sensor is present, the feature is hidden — this is expected behavior.
- Company logo upload (Settings > Inspector Profile) allows inspectors to brand PDF reports with their own logo. If no logo is uploaded, the NexGenSpec app icon is used as a default.
- The "Delete Account" option is in Settings > Delete Account, with confirmation and re-authentication. This permanently removes all user data from Firebase and the device.
- Subscriptions are auto-renewable and managed through the App Store. Users can cancel in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
- The app stores all inspection data on-device. No user data is shared with third parties.
