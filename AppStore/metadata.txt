DESCRIPTION (4000 chars max)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NexGenSpec is the professional home inspection app built for modern inspectors. Conduct thorough property inspections, document defects with annotated photos, and deliver polished PDF reports to your clients — all from a single app.

INSPECT WITH CONFIDENCE
Create structured inspections with customizable section templates (Roof, Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC, and more). Each item tracks severity levels (Safety, Major, Marginal, Minor), observations, implications, and recommendations.

CAPTURE & ANNOTATE
Take photos directly in the app and mark them up with arrows, circles, and PencilKit drawings. Annotations are baked into report images while originals are preserved as untouched evidence.

PROFESSIONAL PDF REPORTS
Generate branded inspection reports with your company name, license number, and the NexGenSpec logo. Reports include severity badges, photo evidence, signatures, and a SHA-256 verification hash for tamper-proof delivery.

HANDS-FREE VOICE COMMANDS (PRO)
Use voice commands during inspections: "Next room", "Add note cracked foundation", "Defect: broken window". Stay focused on the inspection while NexGenSpec handles the documentation.

LIDAR ROOM SCANNING
Capture 3D room scans using your device's LiDAR sensor. USDZ models are saved alongside the inspection for dimensional reference.

INVOICE & SEND
Fill out an invoice, attach the PDF report, and email everything to your client directly from the app. Branded HTML email templates make every communication look professional.

BUILT FOR SECURITY
- All data stored on-device with iOS Data Protection
- Encrypted backup and restore
- Sign in with Apple or email/password via Firebase Auth
- Finalized reports are locked and hash-verified
- Delete account permanently removes all data

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
NexGenSpec is free to download with basic features. Upgrade to Pro for:
- Clean, unbranded PDF reports
- Voice commands
- Priority support

Standard: $28.99/month or $289.99/year
